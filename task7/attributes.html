3. Language and Direction Attributes

The lang attribute specifies the language of an element's content. The dir attribute sets the text direction.

English (LTR): This text flows from left to right, which is the standard direction for English.
RTL Example: This text would flow right to left (like Arabic or Hebrew).

8. Spellcheck Attribute

The spellcheck attribute enables or disables browser spell checking on inputs and textareas.







9. Translate Attribute

The translate="no" attribute tells browsers and translation tools not to translate certain words like code, brand names, or technical terms.

My name is Paladugu Venkata Bharath and I am currently working on a project called html-assessment-Bharath .

The technologies I use are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, MySQL, GitHub . These are technical terms and should not be changed during translation.

My internship company is Paithacs Software Solutions Pvt Ltd . This is a proper noun and must stay as-is in all languages.
